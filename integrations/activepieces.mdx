## Installation

The Supadata integration is available as a piece in Active Pieces. To install:

1. Get your API key from [Supadata Dashboard](https://dash.supadata.ai)
1. Open your Active Pieces dashboard
1. Navigate to **Pieces** and search for "Supadata"
1. Click **Install**
1. Add Supadata API key in Active Pieces connections
