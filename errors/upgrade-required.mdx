This feature is not available on your current plan. This error occurs when you try to access a feature or endpoint that requires a higher tier subscription.

```json
{
  "error": "upgrade-required",
  "message": "Upgrade Required",
  "details": "This feature is not available on your current plan. Upgrade your plan to access it.",
  "documentationUrl": "https://supadata.ai/documentation/errors/upgrade-required"
}
```

## Reasons and how to fix

### Feature not available in current plan

The feature you're trying to use is only available in higher-tier plans.

To fix this:
1. Review the feature availability in our pricing page
2. Compare your current plan with available upgrades
3. Consider upgrading to a plan that includes the feature

### Reach out to support

If you continue to face issues or need further assistance, please reach out to support@supadata.ai, and we will assist you as soon as possible.
